Events and Programs for March

The following events and programs will be held during March and all are free of charge. Geared to a variety of age and interest groups. Check out the many fun and educational possibilities!


Events

All events will be held on Wednesday evenings, from 7 pm to 9 pm, in the Community Room.

  • Milly Johnston reading from her new short story collection
  • Community debate, hosted by local MPP Shanaz Jett
  • Art on Display, featuring paintings by local artists
  • Author in conversation with Sunny Feathers interviewing Gary Troupe

Programs

All programs are held on Saturdays, from 1 pm to 3 pm. While there is no charge, we ask that you register beforehand, as space is limited.

  1. Exploring your roots: research tools for genealogy
  2. 3D printing for fun
  3. Job search skills for teens
  4. Using social media for small business
